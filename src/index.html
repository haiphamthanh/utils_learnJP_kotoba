Study Guide

How this session works

Up
Marks the word as learned and removes it from the current batch.
Left / Right
Moves backward or forward inside the current batch.
Down
Keeps the word in the batch so it appears again later.
S
Stars the word for extra review.
Learned rule

A word can feel familiar after about 5 views, but in this app it is counted as learned only when you confirm it with Up.
